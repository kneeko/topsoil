TOPSOIL

a garden puzzle


Grow plants and work your garden’s soil.



  • Discover seeds
  • Collect birds


In Topsoil, your goal is to harvest the most plants you can! You grow your garden by placing plants next to each other and harvesting them together.




The more plants you harvest at once, the more points you earn. But don't let your garden fill up — that's game over. Keep your soil in good shape, and you'll discover seeds, trees and friendly birds along the way.
